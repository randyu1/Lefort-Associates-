About Us

Lefort Associates is a full service design firm. Our expertise matched with a robust client focus enables us to produce customized designs that incorporate client needs, styles, and budget. We work with each client to craft high quality solutions that satisfy the functional and aesthetic components of each project.

Whether it's a small scale renovation or a complex solution, our devotion to detail and design excellence is our top priority. Projects ranging from playgrounds to pool houses and estates to businesses make our practice multifaceted. We offer a wide range of designs from traditional to modern.
